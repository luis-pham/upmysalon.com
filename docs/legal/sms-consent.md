# SMS Consent

How UpMySalon handles SMS opt-in for review and appointment notifications sent on behalf of salons and beauty businesses.

Last updated: [DATE]

## How Businesses Opt In

Business owners and staff receive SMS alerts for new bookings, missed calls, review activity, and handoff requests via UpMySalon.

Opt-in occurs when a business owner checks the SMS consent option during account setup or activation. By opting in, the owner agrees to receive transactional SMS notifications related to their UpMySalon account.

## How Customers Opt In

When a customer contacts or visits a business served by UpMySalon, the business (through UpMySalon) may send review requests, booking confirmations, or follow-up links by SMS.

By verbally agreeing, providing their phone number during a call or booking interaction, or otherwise consenting at the point of service, the customer consents to receive SMS from that business via UpMySalon.

## Types of Messages

Messages sent through UpMySalon include:

- Review request messages after a customer visit
- Appointment request and booking confirmations
- Booking links sent by SMS
- Appointment reminders
- Reschedule and cancellation confirmations
- Missed-call follow-up notifications
- Business owner alerts and handoff notifications

## What We Do Not Send

UpMySalon does not use SMS for cold outreach, unsolicited marketing, purchased contact lists, or third-party promotional content.

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on customer activity and business settings. Owners may receive multiple alerts per day during busy periods.

## Opt Out

Reply **STOP** to any message to opt out immediately. Reply **HELP** for support information.

## Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.
