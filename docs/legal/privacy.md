# Privacy Policy

This policy explains how UpMySalon collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use our website and our managed digital services for salons.

Last updated: [DATE]

## Who We Are

UpMySalon provides managed digital-presence services for nail salons and beauty businesses, including AI phone reception and booking, Google review management, Facebook/Instagram message management, and website + local SEO / Google Maps services. UpMySalon is a product of **RingBooker LLC**, a company registered in the United States.

For account, billing, and administration data, UpMySalon acts as a **data controller**. For data we handle on behalf of each business (for example call data, review responses, and social messages), UpMySalon acts as a **data processor**.

## Data We Collect

- **Account and business profile:** name, email, business details, login metadata.
- **Operational data:** services, pricing, schedules, availability, staff/technician information, booking preferences.
- **Delegated-access data:** access we are granted to manage your Google Business Profile, Facebook/Instagram pages, website, and booking systems on your behalf.
- **Call-related data:** caller phone number, timestamps, call status, transcript segments, booking actions (via our AI phone service).
- **Review and social data:** Google reviews and your responses, Facebook/Instagram messages and replies handled on your behalf.
- **Website and analytics data:** content we publish for you, and standard site/traffic analytics.
- **Billing data:** subscription status and payment metadata from our payment provider.
- **Security data:** IP, device, session, security logs, abuse/rate-limit events.

## Delegated Access to Third-Party Accounts

To deliver our services, you authorize UpMySalon to access and manage your Google Business Profile, Facebook/Instagram pages, website, and connected booking systems. We use this access **solely** to provide the services you have agreed to, in accordance with each platform's policies.

## SMS Communications

UpMySalon sends SMS messages on behalf of businesses using our platform to support review and appointment workflows.

**Who receives SMS:**

- Customers who contacted the business and provided their phone number (during a call, booking, or visit).
- Business owners and staff who opted in during onboarding.

**Types of messages sent:**

- Review request messages sent after a customer visit
- Appointment confirmations, reminders, and reschedule/cancellation confirmations
- Booking links and scheduling follow-ups
- Missed-call follow-up notifications
- Owner alerts and handoff notifications

**What we do not do:** UpMySalon does not use SMS for cold outreach, unsolicited marketing, purchased contact lists, or third-party promotional blasts.

**Opt-out:** Reply **STOP** to any message to opt out immediately. Reply **HELP** for support. Message and data rates may apply. Frequency varies with activity.
